About Me

Passionate about sharing Japanese language and culture

Your Dedicated Japanese Tutor

I tailor each lesson to suit your level and goals, making sure learning Japanese is fun, easy to understand, and truly sticks. With a background in nursing and healthcare, I bring a unique perspective to Japanese language teaching, especially for those interested in Japanese healthcare and welfare.

My story so far...

Born amidst the golden rice fields of Hokuto town, nestled in the shadow of Mount Fuji's majestic peaks, my journey began in the heart of Yamanashi prefecture. From my childhood backyard, the majestic mountain stood as a constant reminder of Japan's natural beauty and cultural heritage.

After completing my nursing diploma, my path led me to Kofu city, where I served as a nurse. Kofu, being close to my hometown, offered me the perfect gateway to central Japan's wonders — places I had known and loved since childhood. Here, I could share the serene waters of Lake Kawaguchiko with the reflections of the majestic Fuji-san, and the comforting warmth of hoto (ほうとう) — a traditional noodle soup that embodies the heart of Japanese hospitality.

Since early 2022, Sydney has become my new home, where I've embraced the vibrant Australian lifestyle while exploring this vast continent's diverse landscapes 🗿🦘🐨🌿. My recent passions include strength training, the underwater world of snorkeling, and the melodic challenge of learning French — each reflecting my commitment to personal growth and cultural exploration.

Next on my horizon? Maybe the mystical peaks of Nepal's Himalayas, the beautiful beaches of Hawaii, or the lush rainforests of the Amazonas - who knows what life has in store for one?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Japanese tutoring in Sydney

How much do Japanese lessons cost and how do I book?

I offer competitive rates for Japanese tutoring through my Preply profile. All bookings are made through Preply's secure platform, which ensures safe payments and reliable scheduling. Currently, my lessons are priced at $10 for a 50-minute session, and I offer flexible scheduling to accommodate different time zones worldwide.

Book Lessons on Preply

Do you teach students globally or only in Australia?

I teach students from all around the world! While I'm based in Sydney, my online lessons are available to anyone with an internet connection. I have students from the UK 🇬🇧, New York City 🇺🇸, Hawaii 🌺, India 🇮🇳, Korea 🇰🇷, and expats living in Japan 🇯🇵. My virtual classroom makes distance learning seamless and effective.

What age groups do you teach?

I welcome students of all ages! My current students range from 10 years old to 58 years old. I adapt my teaching style to match each student's learning pace and preferences. Whether you're a young learner starting your Japanese journey or an adult professional looking to enhance your skills, I can tailor the lessons to your needs.

What makes you different from other Japanese tutors?

As a native Japanese speaker with a nursing background, I bring unique perspectives to language teaching. My healthcare experience allows me to teach specialized medical Japanese vocabulary, while my global student base (from 6+ countries) gives me insights into different learning styles and cultural contexts. I focus on natural, conversational Japanese that you can actually use in real-life situations.

Do you offer online Japanese lessons worldwide?

Yes! I specialize in online Japanese tutoring and can teach students anywhere in the world. My virtual lessons are interactive, engaging, and use the latest technology to ensure an effective learning experience regardless of your location or time zone.

How do I find the right Japanese tutor for my needs?

When looking for a Japanese tutor, consider their qualifications, teaching experience, student reviews, and whether they offer the specific type of lessons you need. I offer a free trial lesson through Preply to help you determine if I'm the right fit for your learning goals. My diverse student base shows I can adapt to various learning styles and objectives.

What Japanese lessons do you offer for beginners?

I offer comprehensive beginner Japanese lessons including Hiragana, Katakana, basic grammar, essential vocabulary, and simple conversations. My structured approach ensures you build a solid foundation for advanced learning, and I've successfully taught complete beginners from various cultural backgrounds.

Can you help with JLPT preparation?

Absolutely! I provide specialized JLPT preparation for all levels (N5-N1). My lessons focus on test strategies, vocabulary building, grammar mastery, and practice exams to help you achieve your target score. I've helped students from different countries prepare for their JLPT exams.

Do you teach business Japanese and healthcare Japanese?

Yes! I offer specialized courses including business Japanese for professionals working with Japanese companies, and healthcare Japanese drawing from my nursing background. This includes formal language, business etiquette, medical terminology, and industry-specific vocabulary for both fields.

How often should I take Japanese lessons?

I recommend 1-2 lessons per week for optimal progress, but I can accommodate your schedule and learning pace. Many of my students see significant improvement with consistent weekly lessons. I offer flexible scheduling to work with different time zones and busy lifestyles.
